Welcome to Eagles FC

Developing Ethiopia's Football Future

Eagles Football Club Academy was founded with a single vision: to discover, develop, and deliver world-class football talent from Ethiopia. Based in adiss abeba, gelan

We combine modern European training methodologies with a deep understanding of Ethiopian youth culture, producing players who are technically excellent, mentally strong, and ready for the highest levels of the game.
