World-Class Development

Structured Pathways to Professional Football

Every Eagles FC program is designed by certified coaches using modern football methodology. From age 7 to 15, we provide the right training environment for every stage of a player's development journey.

Ages 7 – 10

⚽ Under 10 Academy

Our youngest program focuses on creating a love for football through fun, creative play and foundational skill development. We use small-sided games, colourful drills, and age-appropriate activities that build confidence, coordination, and basic technical skills like passing, dribbling, and first touch.

Ages 7–10 years
Max 20 players per group
3 sessions per week
Birth certificate required
📅 Training Schedule
  • tursday/saturday/sunday : 10:30 – 11:00
  • Monthly mini-tournaments

Ages 11 – 13

🎯 Under 13 Development

The critical development phase where technical skills meet tactical understanding. Players in the U13 program undergo structured positional training, team tactics, and regular competitive fixtures against regional opponents. This is where the real football intelligence starts to develop.

Ages 11–13 years
Max 25 players per squad
3 sessions per week
Competitive local tournaments
📅 Training Schedule
  • saturday/sunday/ Thu: 10:00 – 11:30
  • Saturday: 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM
  • Sunday: Match day or friendly

⚡ Under 15 Program

Our flagship youth program for the most advanced players. The U15 program prepares players for advanced way. Training is intense, scientific, and modeled . Every session is analyzed. Physical conditioning, tactical sophistication, and mental toughness are core pillars.

All Ages

🧤 Goalkeeper Specialist Program

Goalkeeping is a specialized art that requires dedicated coaching. Our Goalkeeper Training Program runs alongside all age groups and is led by former professional goalkeeper and specialist GK coach, Mulugeta Abera. Footwork, diving technique, distribution, and positioning are developed with individual attention in every session.

Ages 10+ years
Max 8 per session
Specialist GK coach
Individual video analysis
📅 Training Schedule
  • thursday: 10:00 (specialist session)
  • Integrated: All squad training sessions
  • Private coaching: By appointment

All Ages

💪 summer Camp

Football fitness is about more than being fast. Our Fitness & Conditioning program covers speed and agility training, aerobic capacity, strength and power development, injury prevention, and sports nutrition education — all tailored to the demands of modern football. Available as a standalone program or as a supplement to squad training.

Ages 10+ years
3x per week option
Certified fitness coach
Nutrition guidance included
📅 Training Schedule
  • Mon / Wed / Fri: 6:00 AM – 7:30 AM
  • Summer Intensive Camp: July–August
