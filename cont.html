FAQ

Common Questions

How do I register?

Visit our Registration page, fill in the online form, and our team will contact you within 3 business days to schedule your free trial.

Are trials free?

Yes — 100% free of charge. We believe every talented young Ethiopian deserves the chance to be seen, regardless of financial background.

What age groups do you accept?

Ages 7 through to Senior level (18+). See our Programs page for full details on every age group and schedule.

How can I support the academy?

Donate via TeleBirr or bank transfer on our Donation page, or contact us to discuss corporate sponsorship packages.

Do you accept female players?

Yes! Eagles FC Academy welcomes female players across all age groups. We are fully committed to growing women's football in Ethiopia.

Can I visit the academy?

Absolutely. Walk-ins welcome Mon–Sat. Call +251 471 123 456 ahead so we can give you a full tour and introduce our coaches.
